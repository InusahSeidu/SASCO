About

Sadia Alhassan, originally from Ghana, is a local entrepreneur and community leader who has made a significant impact on her local community in Georgia. Beyond her business endeavors, Sadia actively engages with her community, advocating for social causes and promoting inclusivity. Her inspiring leadership serves as a beacon of strength and unity, exemplifying the spirit of a true community builder and representative of the rich cultural diversity that enriches American society. Her goal is to provide a taste of home to Ghanians and Africans everywhere
